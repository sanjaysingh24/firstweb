Roller Blinds

These blinds that roll up to cover your window. Our large selection is tested for quality, and measurements and fitting services are provided to ensure a perfect fit and long-term durability. Our roller blinds are also available with electric controls.

VENETIAN BLINDS

Horizontal wood or metal slats are suspended by ladder cords and are known as venetian blinds. Venetian blinds are easily adjustable, they can be raised or lowered to let in more or less light.

WOODEN BLINDS

In today's market, wooden blinds/wood still remain the most popular choice. Wood blinds complement solid wood flooring and furniture perfectly.

VERTICAL BLINDS

In addition to their beauty, textures, colors, and patterns, vertical blinds are very easy to maintain and provide excellent light control. Vertical blinds can be installed on any window size, whether it is a French door, patio door, or bay window.

BLACKOUT BLINDS

The light-blocking capabilities of blackout blinds make them highly desirable in London homes and homes across the UK. These blinds are also great energy savers, as the thick lining prevents heat from escaping - saving you money and helping the environment at the same time.

ROMAN BLINDS

The fabric of Roman blinds pleats when raised. Roman blinds are made by putting corded slats on the back of fabric panels. With the cords raised, the blinds fold into pleats as the slats are pulled together.

INTU BIFOLD DOOR BLINDS

INTU bifold door blinds require no drilling or screwing, so they do not leave any marks on the window frame and are easy to install. Designed in the UK, this innovative and versatile INTU blind system seamlessly integrates with all modern windows including tilt and turn and half glass doors.

DAY & NIGHT BLINDS

With Day and Night blinds, you can influence how much light enters your home.

CONSERVATORY BLINDS

We have a wide selection of roof and side blinds for your conservatory.
